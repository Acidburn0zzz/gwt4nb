Contributing to GWT4NB

Under construction!

There are many ways to contribute without writing any code. These include:

  • Submitting ideas for new functionality
  • Reporting problems
  • Promoting the project on your home page/blog/discussion groups, etc.
  • Improving the content of the web site
It may seem surprising but all of these can be more important than contributing to the functionality directly. Nevertheless, writing code is the most glorious contribution and you are most welcome to do this.
  • Look at the project roadmap.
  • Take a look at the project sources.
  • Pick a defect or enhancement from the issue tracker.
  • Submit a patch.
